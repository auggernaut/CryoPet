100% safety guarantee.

After 10 years of development, CryoPet's NASA developed cryogenic technology has been tested and approved by the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) to be 100% safe and reliable. This is the first ever pet care option that guarantees the safety and welfare of your furry friend.

When life gets between you and your pet.

CryoPet's innovative approach to pet care ensures that neither you nor your pet has to suffer separation or loss. Each Croyo-capsule is customized to your length of care and can be adjusted at any time should your plans change. Your pet won't miss a beat.
